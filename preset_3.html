Cherry Blossoms

Cherry blossoms, called “sakura” in Japanese, bloom around late March to early April in Japan. There are over 100 varieties, with different shades of pink, whitey, and even yellow blooms, number of petals, and blossoming periods. Sekiyama, a variety cultivated during the Edo period, is one of our favourites. Which is yours? Pick a colour and watch the flowers blossom and fall.
